# The Easiest AI Automation Most Teams Overlook: Meeting Action Items

The pattern everyone knows: "Did you finish the thing from last week?" → confused silence → "I thought someone else was handling it" → no one wrote anything down.

This is the action item problem. It's not dramatic, but it costs teams real time and real results week after week.

## The Fix: Record + Extract + Route

1. **Record** the meeting (Granola / Otter.ai / Fireflies).
2. **Extract** action items via AI prompt (who, what).
3. **Route** by email to each person.
4. **Follow up** automatically a few days later.

What was a chaotic verbal exchange becomes a tracked, accountable system with **zero manual work** after the meeting.

## Why This Is the Right Starting Point

Filter: **automate the things that happen daily or weekly first.** Weekly meetings are nearly universal, suffer the same follow-through problem, and the fix needs no custom software. High-frequency pain, simple fix, compounding weekly returns.

## Minimal Version

1. Use a meeting recorder with auto-transcription.
2. Paste transcript into a prompt that extracts action items by name.
3. Send each person their tasks by email.

That's it. No automation needed — just a consistent habit.
