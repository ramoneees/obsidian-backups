# iPhone Air 2 📱, inside Anthropic ban ⚖️, Claude Design + Code 👨‍💻

*TLDR Tech digest — 2026-06-18. The day's main stories across AI, hardware, and software.*

## The Anthropic / Trump Administration Story

**"Anthropic Employees Accuse Trump Administration of Targeting Them"** (11 min read)

- The Trump administration's ban of Anthropic's models has been criticized by cybersecurity experts and Anthropic employees as unfair.
- **150+ cybersecurity experts signed an open letter** calling for the administration to lift the restrictions.
- Employees argue: the well-known multiple protections built into the Fable model to prevent its use for cyber offensive uses is **evidence that Anthropic is being unfairly targeted**.

## Apple Hardware

**"Apple Prepares Second-Generation iPhone Air for Spring 2027"** (3 min read)

- The **second-generation iPhone Air** is now in advanced testing, with a Spring 2027 launch planned.
- Retains current look but adds:
  - **Second rear camera** (ultrawide-angle).
  - **Improved battery life**.
- Powered by a version of the **A20 Pro** processor (same chip coming to this Fall's iPhones).

## Biopharma

**"Uneven Frontiers"** (52 min read)

- AI will make **some** parts of biopharma faster and cheaper, but other facets are restricted by the physical world.
- Recruiting patients, dosing them, waiting for results → still takes time.
- **Drug discovery will accelerate, but clinical development will remain a bottleneck.**
- "The durable position is built around the bottleneck, not in the path of advancing discovery models."

## Mobility / Autonomous Vehicles

**"Mobileye is entering the US robotaxi market with standalone service"** (2 min read)

- Mobileye launching **robotaxi service in the US in 2027**.
- Vertically integrated — uses Moovit mobility platform to interact with customers, coordinate drivers.
- Pilot: **~100 robotaxis** in a yet-unnamed city.
- Scale plan: **~17,000 robotaxis within 5 years** if pilot succeeds.

## Anthropic / Claude Product

**"Anthropic ships major Claude Design overhaul with design system imports, code round-trips, and a fix for its token-burning problem"** (13 min read)

- Anthropic shipped an overhauled Claude Design attempting to fix its token-consumption issue.
- With the update, Claude Design transforms from "prototype toy to enterprise platform."
- New capabilities:
  - Bring design systems into Claude Design from **GitHub repo, design files, or raw uploads**.
  - **Output consistent branding at speed and scale.**
  - Code round-trips (design ↔ code).
