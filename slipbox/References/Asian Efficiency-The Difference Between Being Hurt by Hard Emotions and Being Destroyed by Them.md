# The Difference Between Being Hurt by Hard Emotions and Being Destroyed by Them

Coaching client came back from a 30-day mental health retreat and described a new capacity: "I can feel those emotions now. But they don't devastate me."

## The Third Pattern

Most people fall into one of two:
1. **Suppress** — numb it, push through.
2. **Swallowed** — emotion lands and takes them out.

The third: **feel it fully, sit with the weight, and come out the other side intact.** That's what the client built.

## A Skill, Not a Trait

Emotional resilience is treated as fixed personality, but it's built through practice under pressure — like any skill. The client built it in 30 days of structured inner work under real conditions.

## The Accessible Version

Not everyone can take 30 days. The principle is: **notice what you feel, name it accurately, sit with it for a beat instead of immediately problem-solving or numbing out, then choose what comes next.** Tolerance builds over time.
