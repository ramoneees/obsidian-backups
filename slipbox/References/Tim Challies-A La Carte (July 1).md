## Recommended Reading

### 1. **Rugged Love and Your Prodigal** — Dave Harvey (revdaveharvey.com)
> *“Most of our wandering is routine, even predictable. We envy. We lust. We overeat. We nurse resentment. We grow impatient when life doesn't unfold according to our plans. Then we confess, receive forgiveness, recalibrate, and move forward. This is ordinary sanctification. But there are times when 'prone to wander' takes a particularly obstinate and destructive turn.”*
> *Veja também: [Mothers, Let Your Tears Water the Seeds Planted](https://sovereigntyinmotion.com/p/mothers-let-your-tears-water-the) (sobre pródigos)*

### 2. **You Don't Want to Be Internet Famous** — Aaron Armstrong
> *“Anyone can do it. Even you. Even me. And many people do. There are people that you've only ever heard of—literally the only reason—is because they play to the algorithm. Some have built massive online platforms, podcasts, and social followings. Others have mega-selling books. They got what they wanted. Or what they think they wanted. But here's the problem…”*

### 3. **[Sponsored] Step into the story of Scripture with the CSB Baker Illustrated Study Bible**
Centenas de imagens full-color, mapas e reconstruções ajudam a trazer o mundo da Bíblia à vida, com insights de mais de **200 scholars evangelical cristãos**.

### 4. **Conscience** — Paul Levy (Ministers' Letter)
Sobre o que faz a diferença entre ouvir as palavras de Deus e responder da forma que devemos — e os dias em que tratamos a Palavra como ruído de fundo.

### 5. **Why More Money Won't Fix You** — J.D. Greear
> *“…two myths people often believe about generosity. They're related, but not identical. Myth 1 is that only rich people can be generous. Myth 2 is that only rich people can be happy.”*

### 6. **Seven Laws of Technology & Gaming** — Andrew Noble
Sete leis de tecnologia; segundo assunto interessante sobre gaming. *“A real game has a challenge, a rule set, and a victory condition. A growing share of modern titles quietly drop that last one.”*

### 7. **When Grief Becomes Identity** — Kirstin Black (faithfulparadox.com)
> *“I had someone recently ask, 'Do you think grief can become an idol?' My gut reaction was, 'Yes, of course!' As Calvin stated, our hearts are idol factories. As I have chewed on this question over the last several days, I actually think the answer is much more layered. Long before grief becomes an idol, it first can become an identity.”*

## News Headlines
- **The Passion Translation** caindo em desuso. *“As scrutiny of The Passion Translation intensifies following a high-profile investigation promoted by apologist Mike Winger, a growing number of churches and Bible platforms are distancing themselves from the controversial paraphrase.”*
- Na Inglaterra e País de Gales, **1 em cada 3 gestações é terminada (aborto)** — record. 12% entre mulheres casadas; 37.9% entre não-casadas. Apenas 2.3% das mulheres que conceberam fora do casamento casaram antes do nascimento.
- **Estados estão silenciosamente redefinindo a família** (WORLD, Patience Sunne). Mudanças sutis em leis estaduais sobre parentage, reprodução assistida e casamento estão redefinindo a família e minando direitos das crianças à própria mãe e pai.
